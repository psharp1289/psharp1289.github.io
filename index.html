How do we learn, plan, and make decisions?

The lab seeks to understand how humans learn and plan. We primarily use a reinforcement learning framework to develop algorithmic explanations of human learning and planning. Importantly, we also seek to use this framework to shed light on mechanisms of human anxiety.

I have taken a nontraditional path towards this program of research, and welcome those with diverse interests to join the team. I began in a clinical psychology PhD, moved to a devleopmental neuroscience lab to finish my PhD, and spent the past 5 years in computational cognitive science labs. To do so, I've spent time in Chapel Hill (UNC), London (UCL), Jerusalem (Hebrew University), and now New Haven (Yale).
